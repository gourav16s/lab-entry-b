SUMMARY

All students enrolled into a SEBE course or unit must complete a mandatory zero-credit safety unit as the minimum requirement before entering a practical lab class. Current methods are impractical and time-consuming (e.g. presenting printed out certificate as evidence of unit completion, or checking names against a class list). By using this application, student can check if the student has completed the required safety unit – in either the current or previous trimester – as they enter the lab. The Safety Units are an online unit hosted on D2L (CloudDeakin), with a quiz, which the student must pass to be able to show proficiency in basic lab safety. This web application (app) can:
        1. Displays the lists of labs which required safety unit as a pre-requisite.
        2. Display a list of students who have completed the safety unit successfully as required for entry to that lab (ideally would be able to check class
            allocations to determine which students allocated to attend have/have not completed the safety unit)

OBJECTIVES

The objective of the project is given below in points:

Students can check the status of pre-requisite unit.

Unit chair can also check the status of the students

Less time-consuming and practical

Details of 4 labs are given the website
